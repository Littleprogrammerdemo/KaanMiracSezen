Kaan Miraç Sezen

Rising Star of Turkish Cinema

Biography

Kaan Miraç Sezen was born on September 6th, 2002, in Bursa, Turkey. Coming from a family with roots in the Balkan region of the Ottoman Empire, Kaan has embraced his cultural heritage while forging a path in the performing arts.

His academic journey began at the Department of Performing Arts at Bilkent University, after which he transferred to the Faculty of Fine Arts at Mimar Sinan University. Kaan received his foundational acting education at the October Art Atoll, laying the groundwork for his promising career.

Career Highlights

  • Performs with Istanbul City Theaters, showcasing his stage presence
  • Featured in the play "Ay Carmela" at Istanbul City Theaters
  • Portrays Ali in the popular TV series "Tozluyaka" on FOX(NOW),Can in Dügüm and Cemo in Sahipsizler.
  • Known for versatility and dedication in acting roles
